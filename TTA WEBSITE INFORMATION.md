 Perfect for aspiring young aviators ready to take their first serious step into aviation.



**Weekend Workshops**

Hands-on sessions that bring aviation to life through creative learning experiences.

- **Build Your Own Engine** – Assemble and learn how a piston engine works.

- **Navigation Workshop** – Learn how pilots plan and navigate real flight paths.
- **A Day as a Pilot** – Experience the daily routine of a pilot through simulator and ground activities.

**AVIATION ENGLISH**

*Required for every aviation career — the language of pilots, ATC, and flight operations.*



**Dropdown Courses**

**ICAO Aviation English – Foundation (10 Hours)**

Essential for all future aviation professionals. Learn the fundamentals of aviation communication using **ICAO Doc 9835** as your guide. Build listening, speaking, and phraseology skills used in real flight operations.

**ICAO Aviation English – Advanced (18 Hours)**

Comprehensive training to help you reach your target **ICAO Level 4–6**. Includes **3 hours of personal one-on-one tutoring** for intensive improvement and mock test preparation.

 **ABOUT US**

*Your one-stop destination for aviation education and training.*

**Talent Trek Ascend (TTA)** is a Hong Kong-based aviation organization built by **pilots and industry professionals**, dedicated to inspiring and training the next generation of aviation talent.

From **youth aviation classes** to **flight simulator training**, **airline interview preparation**, and **international pilot licensing**, TTA offers a complete ecosystem for anyone passionate about flight.
 Whether you’re a child discovering aviation for the first time, a student preparing for flight school, or a professional pursuing a career in the skies — **TTA provides the pathway.**

Our mission is to **inspire, educate, and equip** future aviators — not only pilots, but also the engineers, controllers, and aviation professionals who keep the industry moving.

With locally designed **training simulators**, partnerships with **flight schools in China, Malaysia, and Australia**, and an experienced team of **pilots and educators**, TTA stands as **Hong Kong’s most comprehensive aviation learning hub**.

*Built by pilots. Inspired by passion. Dedicated to everyone who dreams of flight.*


 **OUR MISSION**

*To inspire and prepare the next generation of aviation professionals.*

At **TTA (Talent Trek Ascend)**, our mission is to make aviation accessible to everyone — from children taking their first step into the world of flight to aspiring pilots and professionals pursuing advanced training.
 We are committed to delivering high-quality aviation education through real experiences, hands-on learning, and mentorship from industry pilots and educators.
 Our goal is to build **knowledge, character, and confidence**, empowering students to explore not just flying, but **every career in aviation** — from engineering to air traffic control.



**OUR VISION**

*To be Hong Kong’s leading one-stop aviation education and training organization.*

We envision a future where aviation learning is **accessible, engaging, and inspiring** for all.
 Through innovation, collaboration, and authenticity, TTA aims to become a regional hub that bridges **youth education, flight training, and professional development** — shaping a new generation of aviation leaders who will take the industry forward.

*From the classroom to the cockpit — we guide every step of your aviation journey.*
